Shamu Datshi

Bhutanese go to dish

Perfect! Shamu Datshi (potato and cheese stew) is another classic Bhutanese dish, milder than Ema Datshi but still rich and comforting. Here’s how you can prepare it:

Ingredients

  • Mushroom
  • Onion – 1 large (sliced)
  • Tomatoes – 2 medium (optional, adds tanginess)
  • Garlic – 3–4 cloves (chopped)
  • Green chilies – 4–6 (adjust to your taste)
  • Butter – 1–2 tbsp
  • Cooking oil – 1 tbsp
  • Cheese – about 200 g
  • Water – 2 cups
  • Salt – to taste
  • Coriander leaves – for garnish

Steps:

  1. Wash, peel, and slice mushroom thinly
  2. In a pot, heat oil and butter. Add sliced onions and garlic, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add potatoes, chilies, and tomatoes
  4. Add water and simmer
  5. Reduce the heat. Add the cheese and stir gently until it melts into a creamy sauce.
  6. Season and finish
